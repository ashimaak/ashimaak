I am an AutoCAD-certified Landscape Designer and Horticulture Specialist with an M.Sc in Horticulture (Floriculture & Landscaping) from Sam Higginbottom University (SHUATS). My work bridges the gap between deep plant science and the precision of technical design — from ornamental floriculture to site plans and 3D landscape visualization.

My journey spans government agricultural field operations as an Agricultural Officer at PCARD Bank, Kerala, where I conducted farm inspections, managed loan file portfolios, and coordinated stakeholder reporting. Alongside this, I completed a 120-hour Professional Diploma in Architecture CAD (AutoCAD, Lumion, SketchUp Pro) at CADD Centre Bangalore.

I am a published researcher in the International Journal of Environment and Climate Change (IJECC), with a focus on post-harvest horticulture. I have also developed a PhD research proposal on sustainable horticultural practices for UAE's arid conditions — aligned with the UAE National Food Security Strategy 2051.
